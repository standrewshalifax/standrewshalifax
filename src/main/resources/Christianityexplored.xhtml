The course Christianity Explored gives you time and space to consider the big questions of life and to explore the life of the person at the heart of the Christian faith - Jesus Christ.

You don't need to know anything about the Bible.
You won't be asked to read aloud, pray or sing.
You can ask any question you want, or just listen.

Find out more at the Christianity Explored website.

This course will be led by our Minister and will start on Tuesday 9th April and run every Tuesday evening at 7.00 p.m., ending on May 21st. Starting with a light meal the sessions will end about 9.15pm. The course will include an Away Day at the Blackley Centre on 11th May.
