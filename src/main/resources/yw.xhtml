Welcome to the YW Webpage
YW is a friendly group open to all women who want a relaxing and friendly night out once a month.  It's a good way to get to know each other well. New members and visitors are welcome!
Meeting on the first Tuesday in the month at 8pm in the coffee lounge, we  have talks, walks and generally enjoyable company.
