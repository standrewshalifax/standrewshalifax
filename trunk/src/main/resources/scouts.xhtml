23rd St Andrew's Methodist Scouts & Explorer Scouts
Welcome to the Scouts' Web Page

The Scout section is open to boys and girls aged between 10½  and 14 years of age. The explorer scout section is open to boys and  girls aged between 14 and 18 years of age. We do a range of activities  including working towards progressive awards, The Duke of  Edinburgh’s Award scheme, camping, cooking, walking, taking part  in competitions and enjoying what we do.  We meet on Thursday Evening 7.30 - 9.00 p.m.
