St Andrew's Beach Party
Did You miss it? Miss What ? 
The Super Beach Party!
Over 80 young people together with grandparents, parents and friends had a wonderful time enjoying;  Carol Wardley and her puppets, Steve Gore the amazing magician, indoor football with Gregg and David,  fun with face painting and paints with Ellie and Jane, hoola hoops and games with Sue,  the bouncy castle supervised by Sam and Susan, a quiz, hot dogs & chips, luscious scones with strawberries and cream,  cakes and drinks.

And there's more - yes really - a stick of rock for all the youngsters when they left - aren't you sorry you missed it?
